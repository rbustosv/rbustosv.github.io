Franklin Breaks Record

Residents of Franklin city received quite the surprise early Sunday morning when an alert was sent to warn residents about power outages, but also zombies. Residents received the bizarre alert during a power outage just after 1:30 a.m. EDT Sunday morning. Heavy rain was reported in the area at the time of the outage.

"Power outage and zombie alert for residents of Franklin," read the alert. "There are now far less than seven thousand three hundred and eighty customers involved due to extreme zombie activity. Restoration time uncertain."

“We are looking into the reports that the system mentioned zombies,” city spokesman Ben Kerr said in a Facebook message. “I want to reiterate that Franklin does not have any zombie activity currently and apologize for the system message.”

Kerr says 7,880 customers lost power, but it was restored within 30 minutes. He did not mention what really caused the outage.
